Zak's Poached Egg on Toast

Bad Dad Zak's World Famous Poached Egg on Toast!

Ingredients

    1 or 2 eggs
    1 or 2 pieces of bread for toasting
    Mayonaise
    Sliced cheeder cheese
    Salt
    Pepper

Steps

  1. Bring about an inch of water to boil in a sauce pan or fry pan
  2. Once water is boiling, but bread to toast
  3. Crack eggs into boiling water - cook until egg is white
  4. Once toast is ready, spread mayonaise and place cheeder cheese over top
  5. When eggs are cooked, use a spatula to remove eggs from water
  6. Place eggs on toasted bread with mayonaise and cheese
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste

Enjoy once cheese starts to melt!
