Zak's Lasagna

Bad Dad Zak's World Famous Spinich and Meat Lasagna - A Family Favorite!

Ingredients

    1kg ground beef
    1 pack of fresh spinich
    400gm of ricota cheese
    Lasagna noodles of your choosing
    One onion
    Mushrooms
    Shredded mozzerella cheese
    Salt
    One jar of pasata or tomoto sauce

Steps

  1. Chop onions
  2. Sautee onions in olive oil
  3. Preheat oven to 350
  4. Brown ground beef
  5. Chop and add mushrooms
  6. Once meet is browned, add pasata
  7. Add spinich and stir
  8. Add ricota cheese
  9. Layer sauce only on the bottom of a dish
  10. Lay a row of noodles
  11. Repeat layers but add mozzerella cheese to layers
  12. Cover dish with foil and cook for 1 hour

Cut and serve while hot!
