Zak's Steak

Bad Dad Zak's Lunchtime Steak

Ingredients

    1 or two cuts of beef steak of your choosing
    Butter
    Salt
    Cheeder cheese

Steps

This can either be done on a BBQ, stove with cast iron pan, or in the oven!

  1. Heat BBQ to high
  2. With BBQ lid left open, place steak on direct heat
  3. For medium rare, cook for about 4 minutes on each side
  4. Only flip once
  5. Once the second side is done, remove from heat and serve
  6. Spread butter and salt on the cooked steak
  7. Optional - lay cheeder cheese on steak

Enjoy while hot!
